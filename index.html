GrandConnect!

Building bridges between generations

GrandConnect was created to connect children and senior citizens to create a community of empowerment and communication.

Children will be empowered in their learning by reading or presenting to the seniors; as well as listening to the seniors sharing their life stories.

Seniors' life quality will be increased by creating relationships with the younger generation.
